Additional Services

Specialized Treatment Options

We offer a range of specialized therapies to address specific conditions and enhance your recovery

Electrotherapy

Therapeutic use of electrical stimulation to reduce pain, improve circulation, and promote tissue healing. Includes modalities such as TENS, interferential therapy, and ultrasound.

Read More
Dry Needling & Acupuncture

Specialized techniques using fine needles to target trigger points and relieve muscular tension, reducing pain and promoting natural healing processes.

Read More
Kinesiology Taping

Application of elastic therapeutic tape to provide support, reduce pain, decrease swelling, and improve performance without restricting range of motion.

Read More
Vestibular Rehabilitation

Specialized therapy for patients with vestibular disorders affecting balance and causing dizziness, focusing on exercises to reduce symptoms and improve function.

Read More
Ergonomic Assessment

Comprehensive workplace evaluation to identify and address risk factors for injury, providing personalized recommendations to improve comfort and productivity.

Read More
Gait Analysis

Detailed assessment of walking pattern to identify irregularities that may contribute to pain or dysfunction, leading to targeted treatment interventions.
