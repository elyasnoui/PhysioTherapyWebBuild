Questions & Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

How long are gift vouchers valid for?

All gift vouchers are valid for 12 months from the date of purchase. The expiration date will be clearly marked on the voucher.

Can vouchers be used for any treatment?

Yes, gift vouchers can be redeemed against any of our physiotherapy services. The recipient can choose the treatment that best meets their needs from our full range of services.

How quickly will the gift voucher be delivered?

  • Digital vouchers are sent via email within 2 hours of purchase during business hours.

  • Physical gift boxes can be collected from our clinic the same day if ordered before 2pm, or can be posted (delivery within 2-3 working days within the UK).

Can I personalize the gift voucher?

Yes, all our gift vouchers can be personalized with a message of your choice. For digital vouchers, you can add your message during the ordering process. For physical gift boxes, we can include a handwritten card with your personal message.

What happens if the voucher is lost?

We keep records of all gift vouchers sold. If a physical voucher is lost, we can verify the purchase and issue a replacement. For digital vouchers, we can simply resend the email. Please contact our reception team with the purchaser's details if you need assistance.

Can I purchase gift vouchers for a specific treatment?

Yes, in addition to our monetary value vouchers, you can purchase vouchers for specific treatments. For example, you can buy a voucher for an initial assessment and treatment, or for a package of follow-up sessions. Please contact us for more details.
