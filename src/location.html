Getting Here

Transport & Parking Information

Public Transport

  • Underground: Oxford Circus Station (Bakerloo, Central, and Victoria lines) is just a 3-minute walk from our clinic. Exit the station onto Great Portland Street and head north.

  • Bus: Bus routes 7, 8, 10, 25, 55, 73, 98, 390 all stop within a short walking distance of our clinic.

  • National Rail: Euston and King's Cross stations are approximately 20 minutes away via tube or bus.

Parking Options

  • On-street parking: Westminster Council parking bays are available on surrounding streets. These are metered and typically allow for up to 4 hours parking.

  • Car parks: The nearest public car parks are Oxford Circus Q-Park (5-minute walk) and Cavendish Square Q-Park (7-minute walk).

  • Special arrangements: If you have mobility issues, please contact us in advance and we'll try to assist with special parking arrangements.
