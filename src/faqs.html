Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a referral to see a physiotherapist?

No, you do not need a referral to book an appointment with us. While some insurance providers may require a doctor's referral for coverage, we accept self-referrals and will work with you to maximize any insurance benefits you may have.

What should I wear to my appointment?

We recommend wearing comfortable, loose-fitting clothing that allows for easy movement and provides access to the area that requires treatment. For lower limb issues, shorts are ideal, while tank tops or t-shirts work well for upper body concerns.

How long will my treatment take?

  • Initial assessments typically last 45-60 minutes to allow for a comprehensive evaluation of your condition and development of a personalized treatment plan.

  • Follow-up sessions usually run 30-45 minutes, depending on your specific needs and the treatments being provided.

Will my treatment be painful?

Physiotherapy should not be painful, though you may experience some discomfort during certain techniques, especially when treating injured or inflamed tissues. Our therapists communicate with you throughout the session to ensure treatment remains within your comfort zone.

It's normal to experience some muscle soreness for 24-48 hours after treatment, similar to what you might feel after exercise. This is a normal part of the healing process.

How many sessions will I need?

  • The number of sessions varies depending on your condition, its severity, and how your body responds to treatment. Some acute conditions may resolve in just a few sessions.

  • More complex or chronic conditions typically require a longer course of treatment. Your physiotherapist will provide an estimated treatment timeline during your initial assessment.

Is physiotherapy covered by insurance?

Many private health insurance plans cover physiotherapy services. We recommend checking with your provider about your specific coverage before beginning treatment. We're happy to provide any documentation needed for your insurance claims.

What if I need to cancel my appointment?

We understand that circumstances change. We ask for at least 24 hours' notice for cancellations to allow us to offer your appointment to another patient.

Late cancellations (less than 24 hours' notice) or missed appointments may incur a fee. Please see our cancellation policy for complete details.

Will I receive exercises to do at home?

  • Yes, home exercises are a crucial part of your recovery.

  • Your physiotherapist will prescribe specific exercises tailored to your condition and provide detailed instructions. We may also email you video demonstrations to ensure proper technique.
